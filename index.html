Orthogonal Views

In the following section we have shown different Orthogonal views of our product.

FRONT VIEW 1 - When the table is stacked on the shelf .

Our Product's USP in this view:

  • Table is stacked onto the cabinet .
  • Chairs are inserted below the drawers in the cabinet.

FRONT VIEW 2 - When the table is open and supported by hinges.

Our Product's USP in this view:

  • The hinges open and the tabele can be brought down .
  • The chairs are then arranged on it's sides.

TOP VIEW: When the table is open and supported by hinges.

Our Product's USP in this view.

  • When the hinges open the table comes down resting on a wooden frame.
  • The chairs can be pulled out and arranged around the table.

SIDE VIEW: When the table is open and is supported by hinges.

This is the Right hand side view of our product when the hinges are open, table is down and chairs are arranged.
